About MediQuote AI

This project is designed to help users estimate their health insurance charges based on key personal and lifestyle factors. Using machine learning, we analyze historical insurance data to predict costs, making it easier for individuals to plan and understand their insurance needs.

  • Input Factors: Age, Sex, BMI, Number of Children, Smoker Status
  • Output: Predicted insurance charges
  • Technology: Python, Flask, HTML/CSS, Machine Learning (Regression)

The model is trained on a real-world dataset and provides instant predictions. Our goal is to make insurance cost estimation transparent and accessible for everyone.
